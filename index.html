GreenWings

Welcome to GreenWings, an interactive portal where you can explore sustainability firsthand using data from over 2,700 real-world flights and over 350 actual national airports!

Start by simply inputting the ICAO codes of two different airports in the provided text boxes. Next, decide on a reasonable balance between carbon footprint and travel time (remember, time is money!). Indicate your selection using the sliders, and press OK to submit the information and see your customized flight path!

Reflect on how the flight path compares to what you expected. Are you willing to spend more time in-flight to reduce your carbon footprint? Do you need to sacrifice sustainability to get to where you're going? How do your personal decisions affect the world around you?
